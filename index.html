Biography of Muhammad Ali Jinnah

  • 1876-Born in Karachi
  • 1895-Education in City Law School
  • 1904-attended a meeting of the National Congress
  • 1906-joined the congress
  • 1912-attended a meeting of the All India Muslim League
  • 1913-Joined All India Muslim League.So many leaders led their nations to get freedom from British rule.
  • 1926-began supporting seperate electorates for the rights of Muslims
  • 1930s-attended the Anglo-Indian Round Table Conferences in London ,and led the reorganization of the All India Muslim League
  • 1939-Jinnah came to believe in a Muslim homeland on the Indian subcontinent
  • 1940-Jinnah presided over a Muslim League session at Lahore where the first official demand was made for the partition of India and the creation of the Muslim's homeland "Pakistan"
  • 1942-Muslim League adopted the Pakistan Resolution to partition India into states
  • 1947-The independent state of Pakistan that Jinnah had envisioned came to be on August, 14
  • 1948-Died in Karachi due to tuberculosis near Karachi, Pakistan-the place where he was born
  • 1947-First Governor-General of Pakistan

Jinnah earned the title Quaid-e- Azam("Great Leader")

His other title is Baba-i-Qaum ("Father of the Nation")
